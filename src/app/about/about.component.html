What we do

We are bringing numbers into the farming game. By installing our system, Farmers can login to their system and see data visualization of the environmental factors surrounding their crops. This allows for farmers to see how their crops are doing over different spans of time. By doing this, farmers can have a better understanding of the environment surrounding their crops, thus making adjustments and getting better yields.

How we do what we do

This system was built using many frameworks and packages. We utilized things like Angular, NodeJS, and ChartJS as well PHP, mySQL, and EngineX. By combining all of these, we were able to create an embedded system that could collect data, store it in a database. That database is then accessed for authentication, and to get the data for the data visualization. This all is then sent to the client, where it is displayed nicely for the customers to view.
